Japan Auction Sheet Guide

Japanese car auctions use an inspection sheet to report the vehicle condition. It shows mileage, grade, interior rating, and a diagram marking scratches/dents.

Common Grades

  • 5 / 4.5: Excellent (very clean)
  • 4: Good overall (normal minor wear)
  • 3.5: Average (more visible wear)
  • 3 and below: Heavier wear / repairs likely
  • R / RA: Repaired (accident repair history)

Interior Grade (A–D)

  • A: Very clean
  • B: Light wear
  • C: Noticeable wear/stains (common on used cars)
  • D: Heavy wear/tears/smell

Diagram Codes (most common)

  • A1 small scratch, A2 medium scratch, A3 large scratch
  • U1 small dent, U2 medium dent, U3 large dent
  • W1–W3 panel wave / paint distortion
  • S rust, C corrosion
  • X needs replacement, XX replaced

Tip: A few A1 marks are normal. Multiple U2/U3 dents, W3, or many X marks may mean more work needed after arrival.
